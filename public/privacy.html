Privacy Notice

Lineage Theatre is currently a static public preview. Information entered into the app is stored in the visitor's own browser through local browser storage and IndexedDB. The preview does not send uploaded family records, customer profile details, payment status, or movie projects to a Lineage Theatre server.

Do not upload sensitive real records until production authentication, encrypted storage, signed upload URLs, payment verification, transactional email, retention controls, and deletion workflows are connected.

Questions can be sent to info@lineagetheater.com.
